# Acme AI Platform Notes

Acme announced a new enterprise automation platform focused on AI agents, workflow orchestration, and data governance. The release emphasises audit logs, role-based permissions, and integration with existing data warehouses.

The company is also hiring three data platform engineers and one AI safety lead. This is a shift from the previous quarter, when most hiring posts focused on frontend product development and growth marketing.

A recent CEO interview said the biggest bottleneck is not model quality but connecting fragmented enterprise data sources safely. The CEO also said Acme will prioritise regulated industries before consumer-facing features.

Potential risk: the platform still depends on customers having clean permission metadata, which many enterprise customers do not maintain well.
